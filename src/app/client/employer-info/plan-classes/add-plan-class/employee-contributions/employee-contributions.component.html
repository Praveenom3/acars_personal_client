    Employee Contributions
"Pay or Play" rule require employees to offer coverage that is affordable based upon a percentage of each employee's household income. Employers would rarely know this number, so the IRS allows for assumptions to be made called "safe harbors"
     Assume W2 earnings is household earnings (W2 safe harbor)     
     Assume household earnings meets federal poverty line (federal poverty line safe harbor)
     Use employee's rate of pay to determine household earnings (rate of pay safe harbor)
     Do not apply a safe harbor to this plan class
